Inside the college database in PhpMyAdmin

1. For admin login after registration, please refer "admin" table: "a_name" for username and "a_password" for password
2. For parent login after registration, please refer "parent" table: "p_name" for username and "p_password" for password
3. For student login after registration, please refer "student_data" table: "student_name" for username and "student_pwd"
for password.
